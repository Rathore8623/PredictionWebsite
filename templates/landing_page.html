WHAT WE PROVIDE

Health Predictions

Tailored for You

Explore the conditions we specialize in predicting—helping you make informed decisions about your health.

Heart

Heart disease includes conditions affecting the heart, such as coronary artery disease and heart failure, often caused by lifestyle factors or underlying health issues.

Parkinsons

Parkinsons disease is a progressive neurological disorder that affects movement, causing symptoms like tremors, stiffness, and balance issues. It results from the loss of dopamine-producing brain cells.

Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic condition where the body cannot properly regulate blood sugar levels due to insufficient insulin production or ineffective insulin use. It can lead to complications if unmanaged.

Breast Cancer Type

Breast cancer is a type of cancer that develops in breast cells, often forming a lump or causing changes in breast shape or skin. Early detection improves treatment outcomes.

How this works?

The process is straightforward. Users are required to provide relevant medical information related to the disease they seek a prediction for. The machine learning model then analyzes the provided data, leveraging its training and testing datasets, to determine whether the user is likely to have the specified disease.

02

How reliable are the predictions?

While the system uses highly accurate machine learning models, predictions are based on probabilistic analysis and are not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis.
The reliability of predictions depends on the quality of the input data and the specific disease model being used. The system provides a confidence score for each prediction.

03

Can inaccurate information impact the results?

Yes, inaccurate information can significantly affect the accuracy of the results. It's important to ensure that all data entered is correct and up-to-date for the most reliable predictions and outcomes.
